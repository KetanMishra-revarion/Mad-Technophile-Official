Motorola Edge 30 Ultra Review


by Minaketan Mishra

19th September 2022

5 Min Read


Motorola is all set to launch its Motorola Edge 30 Pro in India. The phone comes with a 200 MP camera which is the main highlight. Motorola has priced the phone at ₹54,999 in India and will be exclusively available on Flipkart. The phone comes with a design change and upgrades in hardware as well. We have a lot to tell about this phone so stay tuned with us till the end of this review


Packing and in box

We love Motorola's approach to making environmentally friendly packaging. The box is made up of cardboard and has no use for plastic. Even the ink print used on the box is soy-based ink, so there is no chemical use.


In the box, we get the phone wrapped in a paper sheet, a TPU case, some paperwork and 125W charger. The charger uses gallium nitride. Which led to the charger is as small as a 30W charger and 4 times more powerful than it. The 125W charger is a PD charger which means you can charge your phone and your laptop with it.


The packing is neatly laid out and we wish to see big smartphone manufacturers adopt similarly approach and don't compromise on essentials.


Design

Motorola has been continuing the same design language across most of its phones. This includes the moto G52, Edge 30, and Edge 30 Pro. They looked simple and elegant and we liked them.



But this Motorola phone comes with a different design. The camera Bump has become boxy. The three-camera island design comes with a Saturn glass to protect the cameras and the bump doesn't bother much. The Motorola Edge 30 Pro comes with a metal frame and glass back. The Motorola Edge 30 Fusion comes with a similar design, so there might be chances of seeing this design continue across other moto phones too. The phone comes in two colors starlight white and Interstellar Black. We like the black colour.

Motorola Edge 30 Ultra comes with 200MP Samsung HP1 sensor, and this is the largest sensor you can find in a smartphone and this is the first time we have seen it in action. So when we used the camera for taking some picture we noticed that it showed 12MP in the details. This is because the 200 MP camera combines 16 pixels to one to give you a 12.1 MP photo. So, the purpose of this camera is to get more data which will help you get better picture quality. It definitely has the sharpness but how good are the photos?


The camera provides some really good quality pictures, even the objects that are far away retain a lot of details. In some photos the colour on the pictures come out slightly boosted. Overall the main camera does the job really well and we found nothing to complain about.

Display

Motorola outdoes its competition with one aspect of the display which is the 144hz refresh rate. Which is the most in a non-gaming phone. The phone comes with a pOLED display. The curved pOLED panel measures 6.67 inches in diagonal and has a 1,080x2,400px resolution.
The Curved glass has a feature called the edge light which helps you customize the edge of the screen as indicators for notifications, which is cool. The phone supports HDR10+, which means you can stream HRD content from youtube and amazon. It supports White wine L1 which means you can watch Netflix in 1080P. Stereo Speakers These speakers are loud and the sound is split equally between the top and bottom speakers. However, the bottom speaker has more base. The Motorola Edge 30 Ultra comes with Android 12 and it is probably the smoothest phone on Android 12. There is no bloatware. You get a feature called Motorola Strongbox with think shield security which is a hardware chip for enhancing security


Perfomance


The Motorola Edge 30 Ultra comes with the latest Snapdragon Processor, The Snapdragon 8+Gen1, with 8 GB DDR5 RAM and 128GB UFS3.1 Storage. The phone has managed to level up expectations with its GeekBench scores. It is one of the best-performing Android phones in the market. But it dint prove to be stable under sustained load. In CPU throttle testing the phone performance dropped to around 77% in 3 mins and remained to be at 65% throughout the one-hour test.


Battery

The Motorola Edge 30 Pro comes with a 4610Mah Battery. Its enough to last you an entier day on this phone. The 125W charger will quickly charge your phone. It can charge your phone from 0 to 50% in about 5 mins.

You also get a 50W fast wireless charger with Motorolas own wireless charger and 10W reverse wireless charging. The Phone comes with 13 5G bands that are essential for India and really good 4G+ carrier aggregation.


Verdict

Hats off to Motorola for making a great flagship phone. The phone has everything you need in a flagship and the pricing is very competitive as well. There are some issues like no waterproofing and no 4K recording in ultrawide and Tele cameras. The phone has great built quality, Screen, decent camera, loudspeaker and battery with enough juice to last you a day. There is barely anything big to nitpick and that makes this phone the easiest to recommend
